# Infinite Chess Update 1.10 - Board Editor!

The highly requested board editor is finally here!

## Board Editor

- Place, move, and erase pieces freely to create new positions.

- Selection tool gives you powerful ways to manipulate large groups of pieces at once, inspired by spreadsheet software.

- Fully configurable gamerules.

- Start a local or engine game directly from the editor.

- Save and load positions in your browser or to the cloud (requires login).
