# Infinite Chess Update 1.10.1!

## Arrow Dragging

- Click and drag an arrow pointing to one of your own off-screen pieces to move it directly, without needing to pan or zoom to it first! This is the complement to the existing feature of dragging your pieces onto arrows pointing to off-screen opponent pieces to capture them if legal.

## Board Coordinates

- Added a toggle in the settings to show rank and file coordinate labels along the edges of the board! This mostly benefits the sharing of puzzles, as previous board screenshots had no indication of piece coordinates.
