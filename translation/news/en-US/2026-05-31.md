# Update 1.10.2

- **Move Hints**: When your king is in check and you select a piece, any off-screen legal moves now receive arrow indicators pointing to them, and they will be highlighted when you are zoomed out. This makes it much easier to find what legal moves you have when the only thing you can do it block a long range check via a long range move.

- **Fast Transitions mode**: Added a new toggle in the settings that speeds up all camera transitions. Useful for fast-paced games when you don't have much time left on the clock.
